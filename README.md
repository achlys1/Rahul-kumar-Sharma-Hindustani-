# 🇮🇳 Rahul Kumar Sharma - Professional Portfolio

[![Live Demo](https://img.shields.io/badge/Live-Demo-success)](#) *(यहाँ बाद में अपनी लाइव वेबसाइट का लिंक डाल दें)*

A premium, high-end personal portfolio website designed for **Rahul Kumar Sharma**, an Indian Navy Veteran transitioning into corporate management and rural entrepreneurship. The website beautifully balances his military discipline with modern business acumen.

## ✨ Key Features

- **Digital Tiranga Theme:** A custom-coded animated gradient background representing the Indian National Flag (Saffron, White, Green) with a Navy Blue overlay.
- **Interactive Audio Integration:** A custom toggle button that plays an instrumental version of "Saare Jahan Se Achha" to evoke national pride.
- **The 'Gaurav' Section:** A dedicated section highlighting his 9 years of service in the Indian Navy and his medals (Videsh Seva Medal, Long Service Medal).
- **Fully Responsive:** Optimized for all devices (Mobile, Tablet, Desktop) ensuring a seamless experience for HRs and recruiters.
- **Modern UI/UX:** Smooth scrolling, glassmorphism effects, and clean typography.

## 🛠️ Technologies Used

- **HTML5:** Semantic and accessible structure.
- **Tailwind CSS:** For rapid, responsive, and modern styling (via CDN).
- **Vanilla JavaScript:** For audio toggle logic and interactive elements.
- **FontAwesome:** For high-quality vector icons.
